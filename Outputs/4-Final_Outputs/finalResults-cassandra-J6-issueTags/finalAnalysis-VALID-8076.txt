Description: 

Inserting into the schema created by cassandra-stress.  cqlsh <tt>DESCRIBE TABLE</tt>
says
<div class="code panel" style="border-width: 1px;"><div class="codeContent
panelContent"><pre class="code-java">CREATE TABLE <span
class="code-quote">"Standard1"</span> (  <span class="code-quote">"KEY"</span> blob
PRIMARY KEY,  <span class="code-quote">"C0"</span> blob,  <span
class="code-quote">"C1"</span> blob,  <span class="code-quote">"C2"</span> blob,  <span
class="code-quote">"C3"</span> blob,  <span class="code-quote">"C4"</span> blob) WITH
COMPACT STORAGE AND  comment='' AND  caching=<span class="code-quote">'KEYS_ONLY'</span>
AND  read_repair_chance=0.100000 AND  dclocal_read_repair_chance=0.000000 AND 
gc_grace_seconds=864000 AND  replicate_on_write=<span class="code-quote">'<span
class="code-keyword">true</span>'</span>;</pre></div></div>
but that casing doesn't
actually work:
<div class="preformatted panel" style="border-width: 1px;"><div
class="preformattedContent panelContent"><pre>cqlsh:Keyspace1&gt; insert into "Standard1"
("KEY", "C0", "C1", "C2", "C3", "C4") values ('FF', '00', '11', '22', '33', '44');Bad
Request: Unknown identifier KEY</pre></div></div>
lowercase does work:
<div
class="preformatted panel" style="border-width: 1px;"><div class="preformattedContent
panelContent"><pre>cqlsh:Keyspace1&gt; insert into "Standard1" ("key", "C0", "C1", "C2",
"C3", "C4") values ('FF', '00', '11', '22', '33', '44');</pre></div></div>
